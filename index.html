Community hub

South Shuswap Health Services Society provides mobile lab services, senior support, income tax help, addictions counseling, cyber safety programs, fitness and nutrition guidance, and mental health support for seniors in Blind Bay, BC.

We are a local non-profit serving the South Shuswap region. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of older adults who want to stay independent and connected.

Support paths

Mobile Lab Services

On-site lab services for seniors who cannot travel.

Senior Support

Income tax preparation and benefits assistance.

Addictions & Substance Abuse Counseling

Confidential counseling for individuals and families.

Cyber Safety & Identity Theft Programs

Workshops to help seniors stay safe online.
